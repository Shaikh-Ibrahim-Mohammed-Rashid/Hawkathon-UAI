How it works

Three steps to a consultation

1

Patient joins queue

Patient visits ruralmed.in on any phone or the village kiosk, describes symptoms, and joins the waiting room — no sign-up needed.

2

Doctor starts call

Doctor sees the queue, reviews the patient's health history and symptom summary, then starts a video, audio, or text call.

3

Prescription & follow-up

Doctor issues a digital prescription with medicine availability nearby. Records are saved and accessible offline for the next visit.

Offline-first design

Works where others don't

Only 31% of rural Punjab homes have reliable internet. We designed RuralMed to degrade gracefully — switching between modes automatically so care never stops.

  • ✅ Health records stored locally, synced when connected
  • ✅ Symptom checker works fully offline
  • ✅ Prescriptions cached and printable without internet
  • ✅ SMS fallback for patients with feature phones
  • ✅ Village kiosk mode for shared community access
